Graham Games is an independent VR studio building fan games in the Gorilla Tag locomotion tradition — movement powered entirely by your own hands, where the only thing standing between you and a Scary Baboon is how well you can swing, vault and climb.

Most of the catalogue lives in survival horror: cold, abandoned facilities, flickering fluorescents, and Apes that track you by motion, line of sight, and the sound of your own panic. A few titles trade the dread for a social sandbox — mods, cosmetics, and friends to mess around with. One, Ear Game, ditches the arms entirely and puts you on legs.

Built for standalone Meta Quest. No PC, no wires — just a headset and a facility that wants you gone.
